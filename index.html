STEP 1

Logging In

How to get into the dashboard

When you first open the dashboard, you'll see a login screen. Here's what to do:

  • 1

    Enter the Password

    Type in the password your manager shared with you into the password box!

  • 2

    Click "Login"

    Hit the Login button and you're in! The dashboard will load with data relevant to your team.

⏰

Sessions last 12 hours. If the dashboard asks you to log in again after a while, that's normal, your session simply expired. Just log in again.

STEP 6

Understanding Health Scores

What the colors and numbers mean

Every restaurant has two key scores and a colour-coded status. Think of it like a report card.

📈

Utilization %

How much of the AIO product suite the restaurant is actively using. Higher = better.

💯

Health Score %

A combined score that factors in which modules are live and how important they are. Gives a fuller picture than utilization alone.

The 4 Health Statuses

😊
Healthy  ·  70% or above
This restaurant is making great use of AIO products. Keep it up!
😐
Moderate  ·  50–69%
Decent usage, but there's room to improve. Consider checking in.
😟
At Risk  ·  30–49%
Low engagement. This restaurant needs attention and support soon.
🚨
Critical  ·  Below 30%
Very low usage. Urgent action needed — this restaurant is at risk of churning.

🤔 Why can a restaurant have a lower Utilization but a higher Health Score?

A real example from the dashboard — no made-up numbers.

Utilization
"Raw" score
→
58.7%

→
🟡

Final Health Status

Here's the simple explanation:

Both scores remove Not Required and Not Applicable modules from the calculation — so that's not the difference. The real difference is how much influence each category gets in the final score.

Utilization adds up individual module weights. When large modules (Kiosk, Online Ordering, QR, 3PO) are excluded as Not Required, the Order & Pay category shrinks in influence — because those modules carried a lot of weight.

Health Score uses fixed category-level weights: Order & Pay always counts for 50% of the Health Score, no matter how many modules inside it are excluded. So a restaurant that scores 100% in Order & Pay gets a much bigger reward in the Health Score than in the Utilization.

📐 Utilization

Order & Pay only adds up the modules that are included. Since Kiosk, Online Ordering, QR & 3PO are excluded, it represents just ~34% of the total.

Order & Pay (100% score)~34%
Payroll (0% score — SW Issue)~44%
Everything else~22%
= 39.5%

⚖️ Health Score

Order & Pay always carries a fixed 50% category weight — regardless of which modules inside it were excluded. That 100% score now carries much more reward.

Order & Pay (100% score)50%
Payroll (0% score — SW Issue)25%
Everything else25%
= 58.7%

Why the gap exists for this restaurant — in plain terms:

✅
Order & Pay is at 100% — Every applicable module (POS, mPOS, KDS, Breaks) is fully live. In the Health Score this earns the restaurant the full 50% category reward. In Utilization, the same perfect score only contributes ~34% because several large modules were excluded as Not Required.
🔧
Payroll, Gift Cards, Time Cards, Tip Pooling have SW/Product Issues — Unlike Not Required, SW/Product Issue modules are still counted in the score — they're included at 0 points. These drag both scores down equally, but Order & Pay's stronger category weight in Health Score more than compensates.

💡 The bottom line

Utilization tells you the raw score — useful for seeing the full picture.
Health Score tells you the fair score — useful for judging how well a restaurant is actually performing given what applies to them.

When these two numbers are far apart, it's a sign that the restaurant has many modules marked Not Required or Not Applicable — which is perfectly normal and expected for their type.

🎯

Goal: Move as many restaurants as possible into the Healthy (green) category. Focus your energy on At Risk and Critical restaurants first.

Quick Tips & Reminders

The most important things to remember

🖱️

Click any restaurant row

Opens the full detail panel. This is the fastest way to dig into a single restaurant.

🔄

Data updates automatically

You don't need to keep refreshing. The dashboard quietly updates in the background.

⌨️

Press Escape to close

Whenever a panel or drawer is open, press Escape on your keyboard to close it.

🔁

Reset clears everything

If the table looks odd, click the Reset button near the filters to start fresh.

📱

Works in your browser

No app to install. Just open the link in Chrome, Edge, or Safari — on any device.

🚪

Log out when done

Click Logout (top-right, next to your team name) when you're finished for the day.
